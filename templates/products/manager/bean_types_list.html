How Pricing Works

The bean type multiplier is applied together with the weight multiplier to calculate the final price.

Formula: Base Price × Weight Multiplier × Bean Type Multiplier
Example: $12 × 1.8 (500g) × 1.05 (Coffee beans) = $22.68
