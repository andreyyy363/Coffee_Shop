How Pricing Works

Each product has a base price. The final price is calculated by multiplying the base price by the weight's price multiplier.

Example: If a coffee costs $12 (base) and a customer selects 500g with a 1.8 multiplier, they'll pay $12 × 1.8 = $21.60
